ER Diagram for Canteen Management System

Entities:
- users (user_id, name, email, password, role, created_at)
- menu (item_id, item_name, description, price, availability, image, created_at)
- orders (order_id, user_id, order_date, total_amount, status)
- order_items (order_item_id, order_id, item_id, quantity)

Relationships:
- orders.user_id → users.user_id (FK)
- order_items.order_id → orders.order_id (FK)
- order_items.item_id → menu.item_id (FK)
